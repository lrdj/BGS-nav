Why choose the UK

The UK offers world-class talent, high investment and a robust, business-friendly environment to reliably expand, trade and invest.

Investment Zones

New hubs for investment and innovation with favourable environments and opportunities for your business to grow.

Tax and incentives

The UK has a clear and competitive tax system and offers a range of tax reliefs to give flexibility to international companies.

Talent and labour

The UK is the top-rated major European economy for attracting global talent and has one of the largest labour forces in Europe.

Infrastructure

The UK boasts Europe's largest air transport system, over 100 sea ports and an energy grid supported by the world's largest offshore windfarm.

Clean growth

The UK is leading the race to a net zero economy, with cutting-edge technology and revolutionary research.

Freeports

Strategically located in key shipping and air hubs, these special economic zones offer new investment opportunities.

Innovation

The UK offers global businesses one of the best and most ambitious ecosystems in the world to grow innovative companies.

Sectors

From cyber security to life sciences, and aerospace to food and drink, invest in a sustainable and high-tech future by trading with UK's leading industries.

UK nations and regions

The economic power and diversity of UK regions offer global businesses one of the best ecosystems in the world to grow innovative companies.
